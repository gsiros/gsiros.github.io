About

Research

I work on Security focusing on Distributed & Decentralized Systems and Systems for Machine Learning. My research aims to explore the intersection of these fields, with a focus on building secure, decentralized systems that can effectively handle and protect sensitive data, while also enabling the use of advanced machine learning techniques. I am particularly interested in the design and implementation of secure communication protocols, access control mechanisms, and privacy-preserving techniques in decentralized systems. Additionally, I am also exploring the use of blockchain technology and other decentralized solutions to enhance the security and robustness of learning systems.
